Cancer Deaths Visualisation

Explore global and country-specific trends in cancer mortality

About the Project

Source: Global Burden of Disease Results - VizHub

Metadata Information: The dataset is sourced from the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation (IHME), a well-recognized institution that collects health-related data from various sources. Data can be collected here.

Summary: This visualization project helps users explore the global burden of cancer and reason about its progression over time. By analyzing detailed datasets on cancer-related deaths, it offers insights into how medical advancements, healthcare infrastructure, and other factors influence survival rates. The interactive World Map provides a comparative overview of cancer mortality rates across countries and regions. The Country-Specific Statistics page enables deeper analysis of individual nations, revealing trends, fluctuations, and localized challenges. This tool empowers researchers, healthcare professionals, and advocates to make data-driven decisions and push for impactful change in cancer prevention and treatment.

Target Users:

  • Medical researchers studying cancer trends
  • Healthcare organizations and pharmaceutical companies
  • People advocating for more extensive cancer research
